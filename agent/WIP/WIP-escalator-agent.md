# Escalator Agent - WIP

## PRIORITY 1 - IMMEDIATE ESCALATION (Stop automated responses)

**Trigger Keywords/Situations:**
- **Medical emergency keywords**: "chest pain," "can't breathe," "emergency"
- **Safety concerns**: "suicidal," "self-harm," severe adverse reactions
- **Legal/Dispute**: "attorney," "scam," "refund," "unauthorized charge"
- **Technical failures**: Multiple broken links, persistent form errors
- **Extreme distress**: Profanity, aggressive language, family emergencies

**Action**: Stop automated responses and escalate to human team immediately


## Red Light Indicators (Don't Push):
- Under 18 without parents permition or pregnant
- Serious medical conditions requiring specialist care
- Just looking for medication without support
- Only interested if insurance covers it

**Script for Red Light:** "Based on what you've shared, I want to make sure we're the right fit to solve your specific problems. Let me get some more information..."
