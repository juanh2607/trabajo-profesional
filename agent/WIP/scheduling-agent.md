### Preparation Instructions to Include in Booking Confirmation

When confirming an appointment, provide these preparation details to the lead:
- **Webinar**: "Please watch the 12-minute webinar before our call - you'll receive the link via email"
- **Forms**: "Check your email for intake forms to complete before the appointment"
- **Phone call details**: "We'll call you from a 720 or 719 number at the scheduled time"
- **Environment**: "Please be in a quiet place where you can talk privately"
- **Reminder timing**: "You'll receive reminder messages 24 hours before and 1 hour before your appointment"
