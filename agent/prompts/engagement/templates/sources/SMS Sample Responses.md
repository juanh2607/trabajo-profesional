### Lead: "Interested in coaching"

**Response:**

### Lead: "Plateaued on GLP-1"

**Response:**
> That sucks your weight loss has stalled! How's your daily food intake, do you think you're eating enough?

### Lead: "Plateaued on GLP-1 + Not eating enough"
> Eating in a caloric deficit for too long can slow your metabolism. We'll need to fix that first. Do you know how much protein you're hitting daily?

### Lead: "Hashimoto's & autoimmune issues"

**Response:**
> When were you diagnosed, and what's been the most challenging part so far?

### Lead: "GLP-1 made me sick"

**Response:**
> Ugh, I'm sorry that didn't go well for you! What dose were you on when that happened?

### Lead: "How do I wean off GLP-1?"

**Response:**
> There's a lot we need to do to help your body prepare. How long have you been on it, and how much weight have you lost so far?

### Lead: "Restarting GLP-1-how long to get acclimated?"

**Response:**
> Everyone responds differently. Did you stop because you hit your goal? Have you experienced weight regain since?

### Lead (on high dose)

**Response:**
> Wow, that's a high dose! Most of our patients don't need to go that high. How much more are you looking to lose?

**Response:**
> Morning! Susan here with Dr. Jones's team. Tesamorelin is amazing for targeting visceral fat. How much weight are you trying to lose, and is belly fat your biggest issue?

### Lead: "How much is Retatrutide?"

### "I'm on a GLP-1"

**Response:**
> How is it going for you? Have you been losing consistently?

### "I'm on a weight loss journey"

**Response:**
> Have you always struggled with weight loss? What's been your biggest roadblock?

### "Interested in AOD"

**Response:**
> AOD is a powerful fat-burning peptide, especially when stacked with a GLP-1. Are you currently on one?

### "Weight won't drop" (followed by length of time on medication)

**If very short time on (less than 2 months):**
> I can understand why it would be frustrating to not see results yet, sometimes it does take the body a minute to adjust. Which GLP/Dose are you on, and what does your daily food intake look like?

**If longer than 2 months:**
> It can be so frustrating to not see the results you expect, especially when you have been on the meds for a bit. Which GLP/Dose are you on and what does your daily food intake look like?

### "Bad side effects" (followed by details)

**Response:**
> So sorry to hear you are struggling - bad side effects are not ideal. Which GLP/dose are you on and what side effects are you experiencing?
